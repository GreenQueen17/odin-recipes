Description

This is a red creamy pasta that can be beefed up with lots of different vegetables.

Ingredients

  • tomato paste
  • 30 mL soy heavy cream
  • salt
  • pepper
  • red chilli flakes
  • 450g pasta
  • 1 red onion, sliced
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 200g mushrooms, diced
  • basil

Steps

  1. Place the pasta in a pot with water and salt and bring to a boil.
  2. Cook onions in a pan over medium heat until translucent. Add garlic and sweat for a minute. Add a little salt and pepper.
  3. Add the mushrooms, salt them and cook until soft.
  4. Add the tomato pasta and cook for 5 minutes, browning the paste.
  5. Add the heavy cream, pasta and some pasta water. Stir well to combine.
  6. Serve with basil.
