Description

This is a vegan take on the French Canadian traditional dish: poutine. The main ingredient that has been replaced is the cheese with a non-dairy variety.

Ingredients

  • 3 potatoes
  • vegan dry gravy packet
  • non-dairy cheese curds
  • salt
  • pepper
  • oil

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350C.
  2. Cut the potatoes into long slices.
  3. Oil the potatoes and season with salt and pepper.
  4. Bake the potatoes for 40 minutes, flipping halfway between.
  5. Add one cup of water to the dry gravy in a sauce pan. Cook the mixture over medium heat until it begins to boil. Bring down to a simmer, stirring constantly. Cook for a few minutes letting the gravy thicken up.
  6. When the potatoes are done, place them in a bowl with non-dairy cheese curds over them. Add the hot gravy over the potatoes.
